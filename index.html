FINTRA. Securities Limited ("FINTRA.") has been operating as an intermediary in Bangladesh's capital market to cater the demand set out by institutions, high net worth clients, and individuals since 2019. As a member of the Dhaka Stock Exchange Limited (TREC No. 084), authorized by the Bangladesh Securities and Exchange Commission (BSEC), FINTRA. offers a wide range of brokerage services and investing solutions to enhance investor confidence and trust in the Bangladeshi capital market. FINTRA. is comprised of dedicated and committed knowledgeable experts who ensure that stakeholders receive the highest level of brokerage services possible. The company strives to offer its services at a competitive rate to all clients while binding trust and creating value for everyone involved through its services.

At FINTRA Securities, our TREC certification reinforces our position as a leading brokerage firm in Bangladesh, committed to democratizing access to capital markets. We take pride in being your trusted partner in navigating the complexities of stock trading while prioritizing your financial success.
